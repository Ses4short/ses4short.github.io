Areas of Expertise

📐

Mathematical Modelling

Compartmental ODE models for epidemiology and ecology, including stage-structured fish population dynamics and multi-group epidemic models.

🤖

Machine Learning & Explainable AI

Lattice-theoretic frameworks for XGBoost and Gradient Boosting, FCA-inspired concept lattices, and interpretable predictive models for agriculture and health.

🐟

Ecological Modelling

Modelling interactions between fishing, climate velocity, and marine fish distribution in Canadian waters; climate adaptation in fishing communities.

Epidemiological Modelling

COVID-19, mpox, HFMD, and rotavirus transmission models; disease forecasting and intervention analysis across Thailand and Canada.
